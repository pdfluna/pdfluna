# pdfluna 🌙

**Free online PDF editor that runs entirely in your browser.**

pdfluna lets you view, edit, annotate, and convert PDF files right in your browser — no installation, no uploads to a server, and completely free. Your files never leave your device, so your documents stay private.

## ✨ Features

- 📄 **View PDFs** — fast, smooth rendering of any PDF document
- ✏️ **Edit text & pages** — modify content, add, delete, and reorder pages
- 🖍️ **Annotate** — highlight, draw, add notes and shapes
- 🔀 **Merge & split** — combine multiple PDFs or split one into parts
- 🖼️ **Add images & signatures** — insert pictures or sign documents
- 🔄 **Convert** — export to and from other formats
- 🔒 **Privacy first** — everything runs locally in your browser

## 🚀 Getting Started

Visit the app and start editing — no sign-up required.

## 🛠️ Built With

- [PDF.js](https://github.com/mozilla/pdf.js) — rendering PDF documents in the browser
- [pdf-lib](https://github.com/Hopding/pdf-lib) — creating and modifying PDF files
- JavaScript / HTML5 Canvas
